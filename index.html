Why FieldSight

Unplanned downtime is expensive. Service data is messy. Tribal knowledge is fragile. FieldSight replaces subjective troubleshooting with deterministic reasoning grounded in manuals, physics, and patterns from the field — then packages it into a customer‑ready report.

  • Deterministic by design — strict mode, schema‑validated outputs, ESS (Evidence Sufficiency Score) gating.
  • Audit‑ready — every conclusion has citations and follow‑ups for defensibility.
  • Drop‑in where work happens — embed as a widget, call the API, or use webhooks.

Evidence Sufficiency System (ESS)

ESS enforces a data bar before declaring a root cause. If evidence is thin, FieldSight asks targeted follow‑ups or stops short of over‑confident answers.

  • Transparent thresholds & uncertainty
  • Prevents “hallucinated” conclusions
  • Improves training & handoffs

Deterministic & Observable

Strict mode (temperature 0), schema‑validated outputs, trace IDs, and stage‑level spans. Designed for regulated or safety‑critical environments.

  • p95 latency under 3s, 99.9% uptime
  • Versioned schemas, backwards‑compatible
  • Role‑based API keys & presigned artifacts

  -H "Content-Type: application/json" -H "X-Api-Key: $KEY" \
  -d '{
    "version": "2025-11-01",
    "idempotency_key": "uuid",
    "org": {"org_id": "partner-xyz"},
    "source": {"type": "by_id", "result_id": "rca_001"},
